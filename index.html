About me

About

Hi! My name is Kamil Ramazanov. I am frontend developer.

I was born on March 18, 1998, in the north of Russia, in the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia). In 2016, I graduated from the secondary school there. In September of that year, I entered at the North-Eastern Federal University on the specialty "Fundamental Informatics and Information Technology" in Yakutsk. In the summer of 2017, I found work in a marketing agency in the office as an HTML-coder. After 3 months, I quit. In September 2017, I was leaving the university, because I lost interest and wanted to work. Then half a year was engaged in making up websites on freelancing. In February 2018, I started working in the company MyTona as a frontend developer.

I like to be engaged in HTML-markup of websites, studying front-end technologies and solving algorithmic problems in the Empire of code and HackerRank.
